How We Create Magic
Timeline

Your Journey To A Dream Home

1

Free Consultation

Meet our design consultants online or in our Mumbai studio. Share your floor plan, style choices (white, dark grey, gold accents), budgetary limits, and space usage goals.

2

Site Visit

Our execution supervisors visit your property to measure dimensions, evaluate wall alignments, and capture lighting configurations. This ensures zero sizing errors.

3

Design Discussion

Collaborate with architects to draft initial layout plans. Together, select raw materials (CenturyPly, Hettich hinges, Asian Paints color codes) and gold trim details.

4

3D Design

Review photorealistic 3D rendering views of your rooms. Play around with textures and confirm exact cabinet spacing before signing off the final BOQ sheet.

5

Execution

Our carpentry, electrical, ceiling, and painting teams begin site construction. Supervisors verify progress against 148 stringent quality checkpoints.

6

Project Delivery

Walk through your fully cleaned and finished space. We hand over the keys along with signed 10-year warranty documentations. Welcome home!
